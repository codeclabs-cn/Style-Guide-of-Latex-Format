\section{Comment Style}\label{sec:comment-style}
Use either the \mintinline[breakanywhere,bgcolor=code_bg_pro]{C++}{//} or \mintinline[breakanywhere,bgcolor=code_bg_pro]{C++}{/* */} syntax, as long as you are consistent.

You can use either the \mintinline[breakanywhere,bgcolor=code_bg_pro]{C++}{//} or the \mintinline[breakanywhere,bgcolor=code_bg_pro]{C++}{/* */} syntax; however, \mintinline[breakanywhere,bgcolor=code_bg_pro]{C++}{//} is much more common. Be consistent with how you comment and what style you use where.
